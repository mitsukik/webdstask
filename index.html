Task 1

Website 1

Suggestions

1. Popup modal

- Many users dislike pop-up modals, especially on mobile where the screen is smaller. If the message is not crucial, consider placing it in the slider.

- The modal appears oversized on mobile view; it should be adjusted to keep content within the screen.

Website 2

Suggestions

1. Slider

- The slider is not set to auto; enabling auto-scrolling would allow users to view more information seamlessly.

- Additionally, with the slider not in auto mode, the placement of controls is inconvenient, making it easy for users to miss valuable information. In mobile mode, users have to swipe down to access controls, suggesting that controls should be immediately visible upon entering the website.

2. Updates Button

- The updates button redirects users outside the site, causing inconvenience for mobile users who may not return to the homepage.

- Consider placing the update news directly under the slider; this button could then be changed to "PLAY NOW" for immediate game access.

3. Add Login Button

- Incorporate a Neopet Login Button on the menu bar or in a prominent location for easy access, enabling players to quickly access the game.

Website 3

Suggestions

1. Slider Content

- Utilize the slider to promote events. Visitors to this website are interested in Neopets, so featuring game events in the slider can attract new players and inform existing players about ongoing events.

- Additionally, instead of content leading users away from the website, consider including elements that encourage users to stay and engage with the site.

Website 4

Suggestions

1. Same content

- Since this content mirrors the information in "Explore Neopia," consider transforming this section into a summary of in-game content, showcasing examples of gameplay or game features. Direct users to Explore Neopia for more detailed information.

- In mobile mode, the character's icon appears somewhat small. Users would appreciate seeing the character in more detail to better understand and engage with the character.

Website 5

Suggestions

1. News

- The entire area of the News Thumbnail should be clickable for both mobile and desktop users, not just the images and text.

- Additionally, consider transforming the mobile mode of the News Thumbnail into a slider, allowing users to swipe for more content without the need for scrolling down.

2. Menu

- Consider placing the game icon, such as the app icon, in front of the game name. This could provide users with a visual representation of the game.
