Task 2

1. About Neopets

Suggestions

1. Add Explore Neopia Button

- When introducing Neopets, consider adding a call-to-action button. Users who are interested in the game after reading the page can easily explore further.

- As this is a brand introduction page, consider incorporating gameplay footage of Neopets and other games. Additionally, include event images and photos to convey the game's active and popular status to users.

2. Add Timeline

- Users may be interested in learning more about Neopets history, so adding a Timeline here would be beneficial. Include successful events Neopets has held in the past to showcase the brand's achievements.

- In addition to the Timeline, consider incorporating some basic game lore to provide users with a deeper understanding of the brand.

3. Use Slider for Our Game

- Implementing a slider for the "Our Game" section would provide easier mobile control and better management, especially as the number of games increases.

4. Add Contact Us Button

- Consider adding a "Contact Us" button. Investors or users with interest may want to reach out to know more about the brand.

2. Brand Ambassador Program

Suggestions

1. Opening all the FAQs

- The information appears to be essential. Allow players interested in this program to access all the information by opening the FAQs for them to read.

- Additionally, consider adding an "APPLY NOW" button for players who express interest after reading the FAQs.

2. Use Slider for Current Ambassadors

- Implementing a slider for "Current Ambassadors" would enhance readability for mobile users, especially as the number of Ambassadors increases.

- Consider future classification for different Ambassador categories.

- Ensure the contact button is easily visible by placing it in a more prominent location.
